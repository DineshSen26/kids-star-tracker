About {{ app_name }}

{{ app_name }} helps families turn everyday tasks into a fun star reward system that kids can understand — even before they can read. {{ quote }}

We built this app for parents who want a simple, positive way to encourage good habits at home. Whether it is homework, brushing teeth, helping with chores, or being kind, children earn stars for completed tasks and save them up for rewards they care about.

Each child gets their own task board with big, colourful icons so they can recognise what to do at a glance. Parents stay in control: you add kids, create tasks, set star values, and manage rewards from one family account.

Our Mission

Make daily routines feel like a game — not a struggle. Small wins should be celebrated, progress should be visible, and families should have one cheerful place to track it all.

  • Keep things simple for busy parents
  • Make tasks visual and easy for young children
  • Celebrate effort with stars, streaks, and badges
  • Help kids learn patience by saving stars for bigger rewards

At a Glance

Up to 5 kids per family account
Star ledger for every task and reward
Auto-suggested task icons for kids
Works on phones, tablets, and computers
